About Us

Jelneng Nigeria Limited incorporated on the 18th of June, 2019 with registration number RC:1593091 is an Agribusiness in Jos, Plateau State, Nigeria.

We farm, process, package and sell rice and also offer these services to individuals, farmers and businesses in the rice value chain. We also brand and package rice for Government, organisations and individuals.

Over the years, we've processed over 20,000,000 metric tonnes of rice and still counting.
